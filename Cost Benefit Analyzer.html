Dear Citizen,

Permit me to thank you for accompanying me to the end of this most peculiar contraption: the Simple Cost–Benefit Analyzer. Not forged in the shadowy depths of a cave (though I admit the branding opportunities would have been excellent), but rather assembled through code, caffeine, and a mild disdain for poor financial decisions.

Its sole purpose is simple: to slice through confusion and lay bare the one truth that rarely lies — the numbers. Cold, impartial, and, unlike most politicians, incapable of spin.

In the footer below, you will find a link to its GitHub repository. Yes, it is open-source — a phrase which here means: “you are free to admire, improve, or mercilessly mock the code.” Should you, through brilliance or sheer stubbornness, fashion a version that outperforms mine, do let me know. Even the most caped of crusaders (and the most proper of butlers) must graciously concede when someone else’s gadget is sharper.

Until that day arrives, may your costs remain modest, your benefits plentiful, and inflation… at least less menacing than the Joker.

Yours, ever watchful of both rooftops and balance sheets,
Archit
